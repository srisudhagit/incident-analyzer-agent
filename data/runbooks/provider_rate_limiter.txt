Title: External provider rate limiting causing notification delays

Symptoms:
- Notification delays
- Dropped sends
- Rate limit exceeded from provider
- Retry backoff triggered
- Queue depth increasing
- SMS provider healthy but email path degraded

Likely causes:
- Email provider throttling
- Burst traffic exceeding provider quota
- Retry policy compounding queue growth

Checks:
- Provider 429 rate
- Queue depth
- Retry schedule and backoff policy
- Per-channel health split

Recommended actions:
- Slow outgoing rate
- Increase backoff interval
- Prioritize critical notifications
- Shift traffic to alternate provider if available
