Title: Upstream dependency failure propagated to gateway

Symptoms:
- 502 spike from upstream
- Request timeout waiting for auth-service
- Upstream 503 responses
- Circuit breaker opens
- Retry count increases

Likely causes:
- Upstream auth-service degradation
- Timeouts under load
- Circuit breaker activation due to repeated failures

Checks:
- Health of auth-service
- Error rate and latency for upstream service
- Retry pressure and breaker thresholds

Recommended actions:
- Investigate upstream service first
- Reduce retry amplification
- Check timeout configuration
- Review circuit breaker thresholds
